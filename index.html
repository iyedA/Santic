What is Santic?

Santic is an advanced healthcare companion and robot designed to enhance medical treatment and daily well-being. It integrates machine learning with Bluetooth-enabled smart devices to provide a personalized experience to patients, rest and relief to close ones, and accurate monitoring by doctors 24/7.

How does Santic benefit us?

Santic provides many features, including:
-Medication Management: Automatically dispenses medicines at the right dose and time, monitors stock, and orders refills when necessary.
-Health Monitoring: Tracks vital signs, mood, and sleep patterns, sharing data with doctors for live supervision.
-Diet and Lifestyle Support: Offers doctor-approved meal suggestions, delivers healthy food, and adapts to your preferences.
-Communication and Alerts: Facilitates interaction with doctors and family, and handles emergencies like sudden health issues.

Why is Santic good?

Santic is equipped with a variety of advanced features designed to enhance your healthcare experience:
-AI Integration: Allows for intelligent interactions, mood detection, and live conversations for a natural experience.
-Bluetooth and Antenna Connectivity: Ensures seamless integration with smart devices like watches and motion controllers for real-time monitoring.
-LED Screen on the Torso: Displays critical updates, schedules, and health information in a clear and accessible manner.
-Medication Tray: A smart tray that extends from the side of its head, ensuring easy access to organized medications.
-Programmable Dosage and Alerts: Provides customized alarms and precise medication dispensing tailored to your needs.
-Compact and Intuitive Design: Combines machine learning with user-friendly interfaces for maximum convenience and functionality.
These features work together to make Santic a reliable and intelligent companion for healthcare management.

FAQ

When will Santic be available to the public?

Santic is currently in the advanced prototype stage. We aim to launch it for public use within the next 12-18 months, depending on testing, regulatory approvals, and manufacturing timelines.

-What are the future plans for Santic?

We plan to expand Santic's applications beyond individual use to hospitals, clinics, and elderly care facilities. Additionally, we aim to integrate global language support, increase medical compatibility, and enhance AI-driven emotional responses.

-Who is funding Santic?

Santic is being funded through a combination of private investors, partnerships with healthcare providers, and potential grants from medical technology initiatives. Including: Biat, Esprit, Siemens, Cisco, Ooredoo..

-What makes Santic unique compared to other health devices?

Unlike other devices, Santic combines AI-powered health monitoring, personalized medicine management, and real-time communication with doctors. It acts as both a medical assistant and a compassionate companion, bridging gaps in healthcare accessibility.

-Is Santic safe and approved for medical use?

Yes, Santic complies with strict medical and technology standards. It is undergoing rigorous testing and certification processes to ensure its safety and reliability.

-Can Santic work without an internet connection?

While certain features like live doctor monitoring require internet access, Santic is designed to function offline for essential tasks like medication dispensing and local monitoring.

-Who is Santic designed for?

Santic is ideal for patients managing chronic illnesses, elderly individuals needing assistance, and anyone requiring consistent health monitoring or medication management.

-Will Santic require regular updates?

Yes, Santic will receive regular software updates to improve its performance, add new features, and address any identified issues. These updates will be automated and easy to install.

-How much will Santic cost?

Pricing will vary based on features and subscription plans, but we aim to make Santic affordable for households while offering premium services for advanced needs.

-Can doctors trust the data provided by Santic?

Absolutely. Santic uses accurate sensors and machine learning algorithms to generate reliable health data, which is regularly validated by healthcare professionals.
